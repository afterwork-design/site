                    <h1 id="Vol.47：如何快速在 Figma 中设计 3D 元素">
                        <a href="#Vol.47：如何快速在 Figma 中设计 3D 元素">Vol.47：如何快速在 Figma 中设计 3D 元素</a>
                    </h1>
                <p>Hello，欢迎阅读我的周刊第 47 期。</p>
<p>这周回归上班，没有什么特别的感想，就分享几个 Figma 插件吧。Figma 做 UI 设计确实强，但是要做 3D 元素就比较麻烦了，如果没有其他软件的辅助，就得借助插件来实现了。</p>

                    <h2 id="3D Vector">
                        <a href="#3D Vector">3D Vector</a>
                    </h2>
                <blockquote>
<p><a href="https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1268139850904385517/3d-vector">https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1268139850904385517/3d-vector</a></p>
</blockquote>
<p>快速将文字、矢量图形转换成 3D 效果，可以修改厚度、角度和颜色等参数。</p>
<p><img src="../assests/weekly/image/47-3d-vector.png" alt="3D Vector"></p>

                    <h2 id="Fast Isometric">
                        <a href="#Fast Isometric">Fast Isometric</a>
                    </h2>
                <blockquote>
<p><a href="https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1249759048471403961/fast-isometric">https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1249759048471403961/fast-isometric</a></p>
</blockquote>
<p>快速创建立方体，预设 4 种角度，支持修改立方体尺寸和颜色。</p>
<p><img src="../assests/weekly/image/47-fast-isometric.jpg" alt="Fast Isometric"></p>

                    <h2 id="Shapes">
                        <a href="#Shapes">Shapes</a>
                    </h2>
                <blockquote>
<p><a href="https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1256475795987389620/shapes">https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1256475795987389620/shapes</a></p>
</blockquote>
<p>快速生成预设形状的体块,支持修改参数。</p>
<p><img src="../assests/weekly/image/47-shapes.png" alt="Shapes"></p>

                    <h2 id="Shapes 3D">
                        <a href="#Shapes 3D">Shapes 3D</a>
                    </h2>
                <blockquote>
<p><a href="https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1266663542116780385/shapes-3d">https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1266663542116780385/shapes-3d</a></p>
</blockquote>
<p>快速将矢量图形转换成 3D 效果，预设镜面、磨砂、玻璃、金属等多种渲染效果。</p>
<p><img src="../assests/weekly/image/47-shapes-3d.png" alt="Shapes 3D"></p>

                    <h2 id="Simple3D">
                        <a href="#Simple3D">Simple3D</a>
                    </h2>
                <blockquote>
<p><a href="https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1260632020336566834/simple3d">https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1260632020336566834/simple3d</a></p>
</blockquote>
<p>类似 Adobe Illustrator 的 3D 功能。</p>
<p><img src="../assests/weekly/image/47-simple3d.png" alt="Simple3D"></p>

                    <h2 id="3D Transformer">
                        <a href="#3D Transformer">3D Transformer</a>
                    </h2>
                <blockquote>
<p><a href="https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1009780509659307229/3d-transformer">https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1009780509659307229/3d-transformer</a></p>
</blockquote>
<p>支持调整图片或 frame 的角度、比例、圆角、透视程度。</p>
<p><img src="../assests/weekly/image/47-3d-transformer.jpg" alt="3D Transformer"></p>

                    <h2 id="Fig3D">
                        <a href="#Fig3D">Fig3D</a>
                    </h2>
                <blockquote>
<p><a href="https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/946020080871644950/fig3d">https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/946020080871644950/fig3d</a></p>
</blockquote>
<p>能调整光影渲染效果的 3D 插件。</p>
<p><img src="../assests/weekly/image/47-fig3d.png" alt="Fig3D"></p>
